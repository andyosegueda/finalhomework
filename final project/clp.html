Getting your commercial learners permit

The first step in this process is to decide whether you want to do a training program with a mega carrier or any company that would pay for your CDL, or pay all the expenses with your own money
Important to note is that companies will make you work for them 6 months to a year after getting your CDL or you will be be charged a penalty fee. You can also buy out of the agreement if you prefer
I paid for everything myself so I will be explaining the process from that perespective
                                                                      If you decide to pay everything yourself
Go to the DMV with your 6 points of ID and tell them you want to get a commercial learners permit
You will have to pay $125 inorder to get the receipt for your permit
After getting your reciept you will have to go online to New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission website
To prepare for your tests you can study the CDL manual availabe at the DMV or what I did was watch youtube videos to study the questions (they're the same questions)
To get your CDL you must pass 3 tests at the DMV (general knowledge, air brakes, and combination)
      General Knowledge consists of 50 questions and you must get 40 correct or you fail
      Air brakes consists of 25 questions and you must get 20 correct or you fail
      Combination consists of 25 questions and you must get 20 correct or you fail
If you fail a test you must wait at least 2 weeks to retake it and reschedule an appointment online
After passing all 3 you will to the clerk and they will validate your results
You can also add endorsements to your license at this stage
The endorsements that you can add to your license are doubles/triples, tanker, HAZMAT, and passenger
After passing all 3 you will be required to go get a physical and submit it to the DMV. You can go to any clinic and have them preform a physical, and after you will issued a medical certificate. The certificate is valid for 2 years and you must renew every 2 years
* Also important to note that for the DMV your eye sight needs to be at least 20/50 but to pass a DOT physcial you need at least 20/40 vision to pass
Once they have your test scores and your medical certifciate you will be issued your permit

                                                             At this stage you're ready to go to a driving school
