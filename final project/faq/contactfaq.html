How old do you need to be to get your license and what are the requirements? +

You have to be at least 18 years old and possess a license from the state that you currently live in

How much does driving school cost? +

There is no set cost to how much it will cost you but it can range anywhere from 3000 to 10000 dollars

I dont know how to drive manual is that a problem? +

This is a situational thing because most trucks nowadays from the assembly line are automatic, but some jobs will have manual trucks. It's also important to note if you take your test in a automatic truck you will get a manual restriction meaning you cannot legally operate a manual vehicle.

What is the hardest thing about driving a class a vehicle? +

Having to share the road with four wheelers
